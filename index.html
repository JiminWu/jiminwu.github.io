I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher at Rice University in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and Bioengineering, co-advised by Prof. Jacob T. Robinson and Prof. Ashok Veeraraghavan.

My research interests involve the integration of computational imaging, microscopy, neuroengineering, medical imaging and machine learning. My current research focuses on leveraging AI-enabled optics optimization and imaging algorithms to overcome the challenges of traditional lens-based systems and develop computational microscopes with superior imaging performance, enabling large-scale neural signal recording and enhanced medical imaging capabilities.
